Learning page name can't be changed once assigned. What is Learning page name?

Learning Page

The Learning page name should reflect the name of your institution or your brand. Your learning page will have the custom url: www.flinnt.com/yourlearningpagename/

What is learning page?

It is the gateway from where learners can discover and join your blended as well as online learning courses. It helps you engage with your existing learners and connect with prospective learners.

What is displayed on the Page?

It gives a summary of the courses, number of learners and how active is your digital learning community. The page displays your private, public as well as paid courses. Obviously the private courses remain private, users cannot directly join them from here, they can at the most send you a request so that you can vet their request and permit them to join on merit.

What can I do with the Flinnt Learning Page?

You can promote this page to get existing and prospective learners to learn with you. Users can search for your institution or your courses, showcase the teaching learning expertise that you have and enable a large number of learners to benefit from it.

Have Questions to ask?

What is Flinnt?
Flinnt is an instant communication & sharing platform designed for Educational Institutions, aiming to make learning exciting & engaging. The high degree of control along with the academic structure makes Flinnt the preferred choice of educational institutions. Flinnt is device agnostic and can be accessed through the website as well as the android and iOS app.
What kind of educational institutions use Flinnt?
A variety of educational institutions; Schools, Colleges, Pre-Schools and Coaching Institutions are using Flinnt to communicate day to day happenings and to share learning resources with Learners, Parents and Teachers.
Is Flinnt a Free Platform?
Flinnt is absolutely free for Educational Institutions, they can create unlimited number of courses and they can also register unlimited number of learners, teachers and parents. Needless to say it is free for Learners, Teachers and Parents as well.
Is there a way to try out Flinnt?
Create a few courses and register a particular class of learners or a group of teachers and you are on your way! Once you are comfortable with the platform you can then add more courses and register all the learners and teachers of your institution.
Where can we find out more about how Flinnt is being implemented?
Read it directly from the Horse’s mouth in the institutional Case Studies or explore our blog.
How to Implement Flinnt at an Institution level?
  • Step 1 Sign up as an Institution
  • Step 2 Create Courses & allocate them to teachers.
  • Step 3 Teachers to register & Join Courses through Web or App
  • Step 4 Teachers to start communicating & sharing: App Posting or Web Posting
  • Step 5 Learners & Parents Join Courses through Web or App
What controls are available to Institutions and Educators on Flinnt?
Posting rights are allocated to the institution Admin and the Teacher leading the Course. Learners can be removed, transferred to another course or even switched to a read only mode. Teachers have full rights to control who can comment and who can’t, they can even delete any inappropriate comments made by any user on their course.
Any other benefits to the Institution?
Other than bringing down the cost of communication and also making it seamless and standardized, Flinnt ensures that a record of the academic resources that are shared in a subject and all the interactions between the teacher and the learners in retained by the institution. When a Teacher leaves a course, all the past records can be accessed by the incoming teacher, this ensures that a continuity can be ensured.
