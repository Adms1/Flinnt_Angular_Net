About flinnt

Flinnt is India's most exciting learning app for K-10 students. On Flinnt, students can learn from free and paid courses mapped to CBSE, NCERT, NSO, IMO, IEO, ASSET, and other Olympiads.

What keeps student excited in learning is our communication channel in each course which shows the connection between the real world & the subject topics being learned at School on an everyday basis.

These courses are helpful for strengthening the subject concepts as well as mastering exam preparation. The study material provided in these courses consist of online videos, quizzes, MCQ, test series, worksheets, notes, online links & images.

Flinnt is also used by Schools, Colleges, and Kindergartens for communication and sharing of learning resources.
